PLANT based

At Alokino we create handcrafted, plant-based gelato from scratch, using only the highest quality, natural, raw ingredients that we can source. There are no fillers, highly processed or semi-finished ingredients in any of our recipes. The secret of our gelato is the use of our innovative Balancing Method that requires a deep understanding of the natural properties of each ingredient, combined with an artisanal craftmanship approach. The result is an all-natural gelato with a deliciously creamy texture, made of carefully selected ingredients that you can feel good about.

No guilt babe, just gelato.
